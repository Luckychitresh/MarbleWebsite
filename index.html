About Our Legacy

A Heritage of Excellence in
Natural Stone

Versatile Stonex is a distinguished family-owned marble trading and export company rooted in the heart of Rajasthan, India — the marble capital of the world. For over two decades, we have been sourcing, processing, and supplying the finest natural stones to discerning clients across India and the globe.

Our journey began with a passion for natural stone and a vision to bring India's magnificent marble heritage to the world. Today, we stand as one of the most trusted names in the industry, serving top builders, architects, real estate developers, and international importers with an unwavering commitment to quality.

Direct Quarry Sourcing

Premium stones sourced directly from the finest quarries in Rajasthan, South India, and Italy.

Global Export Vision

Serving clients in 5+ countries with export-grade packaging and seamless logistics.

Quality Assurance

Every slab inspected for veining, color consistency, thickness, and structural integrity.
